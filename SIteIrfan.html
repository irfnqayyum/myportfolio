About Me

Hello! I'm Muhammad Irfan Qayyum Bin Shazali @ Azamannizam

Background & Education: I am currently pursuing my studies in Bachelor of Cyber Security at UniKL MIIT, where I am developing my passion for all things cyber security as I always wanted to be a hacker. My journey into Internet security began with my fascination when watching the movie "Blackhat", it immediately peaked my interest for hacking. It is very fascinating how a person can gain access into the system by just pressing few buttons on a keyboard.

Philosophy: I believe that to do anything you need to have passion for it in order to do it well. We must also seek help from the The Almighty Creator, Allah SWT.

Skills & Approach: I focus on responsive design, user-centered thinking, and accessibility compliance. My design process always starts with understanding the user's needs and business objectives, followed by research, wireframing, prototyping, and iterative testing to ensure optimal results.

Future Goals: As i continue to grow in this field, I aim to be a jack of all trades in Cyber Security but also make a positive impact on people's lives especially my family.
